Lennox Lewis: Former Undisputed Heavyweight Boxing Champion


Brief Introduction

Lennox Lewis is a retired British-Canadian boxer who was one of the dominant heavyweight champions of the 1990s and early 2000s. He was born on September 2, 1965, in West Ham, London, England, and grew up in Canada. Lewis won a gold medal at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea, and turned professional shortly thereafter. He held various world titles during his career, including the WBC, IBF, and WBA heavyweight titles. Lewis retired with a record of 41 wins, 2 losses, and 1 draw, and is considered one of the greatest heavyweight boxers of all time. He was inducted into the International Boxing Hall of Fame in 2009.
