Mike Tyson is considered one of the greatest boxers of all time, known for his incredible power and ferocious style in the ring. He was the youngest heavyweight champion in history at the age of 20 and held the undisputed heavyweight championship for several years. Tyson's record boasts 50 wins, with 44 of those wins coming by knockout. His boxing style and intimidating persona inside and outside the ring made him a pop culture icon.
